Connected teams. Data-informed decisions. Automated operations.

Predictive Insights Center (PIC) is the critical enabler for Takeda’s digital transformation across GMS/GQ: a single, AI-ready platform that connects data, tools, and teams for holistic oversight, foresight, and insight across the end-to-end product journey.
